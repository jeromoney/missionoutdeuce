Why buyers care

MissionOut is designed around operational failure modes, not marketing checklists.

Reliability

Native mobile alert handling starts the alarm path even when Flutter is not running.

Critical alerts are owned by Android and iOS native layers so the app can wake cold-started devices, play alarm audio, vibrate, and present immediate responder actions under stress.

Control

Dispatchers get a fast web workflow without carrying team-management complexity.

MissionOut separates dispatcher, team admin, and super admin responsibilities so each role sees only the workflows required to do their job accurately during live incidents.

Accountability

Every incident, delivery attempt, and response action is written into a clear operational record.

Leadership can review what was sent, which devices were targeted, who responded, and when escalation occurred without reconstructing events from multiple disconnected systems.
