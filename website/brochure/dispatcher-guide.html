Dispatcher workflow

Dispatchers move from incident details to live acknowledgement tracking without leaving the operational screen.

MissionOut keeps the dispatcher interface fast, focused, and role-specific so incident creation and response monitoring stay clear during time-critical missions.

Step by step

How to use the dispatcher workflow.

Step 1

Create the incident

Choose the team, set mission priority, add the location, and capture the notes responders need immediately.

Step 2

Launch the alert

Submit the incident to start device targeting and initialize the delivery and acknowledgement record.

Step 3

Watch acknowledgement status live

Monitor who is responding, who is unavailable, and who has not yet acknowledged.

Step 4

Adapt as the mission develops

Use incoming status to decide whether enough responders are committing or escalation needs to continue.

Why it matters

Fast launch

Clear status

Operational confidence
